### The Three-Act Lecture From Scratch

#### Act I: The Dissection of Core  

sometimes educational purposes demand hard decisions. microKarphaty takes what you said, strips it down to the bone and then truns it through a brutal tokenization process:  

- stopwords, numbers, capitalization: rejected.  
- pinctuation: never heard of her.  
- core words selected by length, rarity, position, and a sprinkle of chaos: noway.
- single letters: discarded.

what remains to microKarpathy after all this? only the the words that *matter* вЂ” or at least, the words that think they do. for each core word, microKarphaty grows a recursive branching tree of mutations. the mutation provider works by **embedding cosine similarity**: given a word, tokenize it, look up its embedding vector, compute cosine distance against all vocabulary tokens and return the nearest neighbors, because good boring relations with neighbors are a recipe for prosperity and enlightness.  

microKarpathy's tree is a tree where each word branches into `width` children, recursively, up to `depth` levels. the levels that are closests ones to hell.
